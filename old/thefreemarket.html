The Free Market

If there is one thing that humanity has learned, it’s that there is a price to anything. With this in mind, the free market was formed. Freedom fulfilled through a market without morals or spiritual hang-up’s, everything and anything for sale to those with the money. Where chaos would be, the founders took refuge in a new built mall without any real supplies and traded their way to harmony and profit. There is only way rule in the Free Market; Never break a deal. The founders left their legacy alive and well under the free council, the leaders ensure that no one steps too far out of line, though there has always been rumors of a deep underground of those traders willing to deal in…less savory things still protected under the freedom of trade.

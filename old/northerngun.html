Northern Gun

Built upon an old munitions factory, Northern Gun is known for its wealth of arms and its distinct old west feel. The cowboys and cow gals make their wealth by holding their ranges, breeding cows, goats and the likes. The Rangers of Northern Gun are another well-known force, good folks ready and willing dispense justice and punish wrong doers with a steady aim, quick reflexes and a red right hand.
